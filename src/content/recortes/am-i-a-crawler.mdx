i just had an existential thought:

<Lede>am i a crawler or are crawlers like me?</Lede>  i'll explain.

when i find a blog i like, i will trawl through their archives and read through all the cool new stuff i've found. i'll save some links [on my pinboard](https://pinboard.in/u:lukealexdavis) and may blog about them as well/instead. that sounds like the behaviour of a crawler. they go through sites that they find and build up the search engine's index with new pages they find or update the ones already there.

so who/what is emulating who/what? is this by design or is there a mimic effect... in effect?

recently, i've started likening my brain to an index because i'll remember what i've written on a blog before thinking about searching it up on the world wide web. it's partly _why_ i put things on my blogs—so i can remember them. being able to retrieve information like that is helpful but it's not transferrable to someone else. i can't imagine or expect any/everyone to think like that. it makes potential collabs hard but luckily i don't work with other people outside of work. it's always on my mind though.

alas, i digress.
