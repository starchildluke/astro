## Criteria

* They must be deemed a Christmas movie by my definition. I really don't care if you think Die Hard is or isn't a Christmas movie. Go touch grass.
* Films with more than 1 watch logged between 2018 or films that I remmeber watching more than once are eligible.
* The film must have an average rating of 3 or more
