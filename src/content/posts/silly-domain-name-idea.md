Someone at work mentioned that they going to bring in a blueberry-related baked good as part of their work anniversary later this month and my autistic brain followed this process:

1. Look for the you forgot blueberries meme in GIF form
2. Look for an alternatively funny meme since it wasn't there
3. Realise that I keep responding to work chat messages with memes and worry that it's annoying
4. Remember the actual meme and the title being [YouForgotBlueberries.com](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yqEeP1acj4Y)
5. Checking YouForgotBlueberries.com and apparently getting blocked from accessing it
6. Checking Domainr to see if it was taken (yes, it was)
7. Wondering if any alternatives exist
8. Finding youforgotblueberri.es which _is_ available

And here we are. I am seriously considering getting youforgotblueberri.es and just posting the video embed as the homepage. The domain is relatively cheap so it wouldn't be the worst annual payment I make. Maybe I could add more pages about blueberries themselves for SEO purposes and see how well it would do (it is my profession after all).
